# Currency image credits

United States banknotes (except the $100 note) and coins are works of the
U.S. federal government and are not subject to copyright, so those are
**public domain**. The USD $100 note is a higher-resolution Wikimedia
Commons photograph released under **CC BY-SA 4.0**, which requires
attribution. EUR note images are official ECB reproductions and PLN note
images are official NBP specimen ("WZÓR") images — both are **© their
respective central bank** and are **NOT public domain**; they are used here
under each bank's stated reproduction terms, not as freely licensed works.

Coin images were trimmed and re-centered onto a transparent square so the
renderer can clip them to a circle; note images are unmodified (or
minimally cropped) versions of the source images.
